Who We Are

Siganalianz Security Hub (SSH) is a dedicated cybersecurity society focused on educating and challenging security enthusiasts through Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions.

Our goal is to provide a platform for students and professionals to enhance their penetration testing, cryptography, reverse engineering, and forensics skills.

  • 📅 Duration: 10 hours
  • 💡 Categories: Web, Forensics, Crypto, Cloud, Reverse Engineering
